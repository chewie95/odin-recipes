Créme Brulèe

Description

This traditional French dessert is pairs excellently with a good wine.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Whipped Cream
  • Sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Vanilla pods or vanilla escence

Steps

  1. Put the whipped cream and milk on a pan on low heat and let boil. Once it boils, turn the heat off and let cool for 10-15 minutes until warm
  2. In the meantime, mix 4 egg yolks and all the sugar gently until most of the sugar is dissolved
  3. Once the cream and milk mixture is warm, pour a little bit while filtering onto the eggs yolks and sugar
  4. Mix gently without generating any froth. Once all the sugar is dissolved, pour the rest of the cream and sugar, again, while filtering
  5. Pour the mixture onto the recipients
  6. Cook for 45-60 minutes at 100°C
  7. Let cool and put in fridge 2-3 hours
  8. Sparkle brown sugar on top and burn with the help of a blowtorch
