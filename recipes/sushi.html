Sushi

Description

High end Japanese cuisine, mastered through great skill and patience

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salmon
  • Avocado
  • Philadelphia cheese
  • Kombu
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Wash the rice 7 to 8 times stirring thoroughly to take out all the starch
  2. Cook the rice in a pan with water just above the rice level for 22 minutes
  3. Prepare salt, sugar and vinegar mixture
  4. Once cooked, put the rice in a bowl and while hot, pour the salt, sugar and vinegar mixture
  5. Let cool for about an hour
  6. Once cooled, put the kombu and put rice grains 3/4 up to its full length
  7. Place the salmon, Philadelphia cheese and avocado parts
  8. Roll the kombu and close it
  9. With a wet knife, cut it and serve
